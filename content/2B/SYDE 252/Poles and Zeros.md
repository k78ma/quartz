### Laplace / Continuous Time
- $H(s)$ has a pole at $s=a$ if $| H(a) |=\infty$
- $H(s)$ has a zero at $s=a$  if $| H(a) |=0$

### Z-transform / Discrete Time
- $H[z]$ has a pole at $z=a$ if $| H(a) |=\infty$
- $H[z]$ has a zero at $s=a$  if $| H[a] |=0$

Poles: $x$
Zeros: $0$
