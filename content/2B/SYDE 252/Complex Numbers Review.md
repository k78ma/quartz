Euler formula:
$$
\cos \theta + j\sin \theta=e^{j\theta}
$$
Euler identity:
$$
\begin{align}
\theta&=\pi \\
e^{j\pi}&=\cos \pi + j\sin \pi \\
e^{ j\pi }&=-1 \\
e^{ j\pi }+1&=0
\end{align}
$$
---
$$
\begin{align}
z=r(\cos \theta+j\sin \theta)=r e^{ j\theta } \\
z= \mid z \mid e^{ j \angle z }
\end{align}
$$
