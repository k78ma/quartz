Very similar conceptually to [[RC Formulation]] and [[RC Circuit Behaviour]], except we are working with inductors and current.

We have:
$$
\tau = \frac{L}{R}
$$
such that
$$
I(t) = I_{0}^{-\frac{t}{\tau}}
$$
